About This Unit

This elegant 1 Bedroom Urban Suite offers the perfect blend of style, comfort, and functionality. Designed with the modern urban dweller in mind, this 65 m² residence features premium finishes, an open-concept layout, and floor-to-ceiling windows that flood the space with natural light.

The gourmet kitchen is equipped with high-end stainless steel appliances, quartz countertops, and custom cabinetry. The spacious living area opens onto a private balcony with stunning city views, perfect for morning coffee or evening relaxation.

The bedroom features a walk-in closet and an en-suite bathroom with a rainfall shower, heated floors, and designer fixtures. Additional features include smart home technology, energy-efficient appliances, and soundproof walls for ultimate privacy.
